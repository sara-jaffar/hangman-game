How To Play

1. look at the blanks to see how many letters are in the word.

2. Type a letter to guess:
- ✅ If it is correct, it appears in the word.
- ❌ If it is incorrect, the hangman drawing begins.

3. You have limited number of trials:
- Each wrong guess adds a part to the stick figure.

4. Keep guessing letters until:
- 🏆 You reveal the whole word (you win!)
- 💀 The hangman is fully drawn (you lose!)
